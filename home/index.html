ABOUT ME

Hi there! I am a first year PhD student at the Universiy of Minnesota advised by Stevie Chancellor. I am interested at the intersection of Machine Learning and Human Computer Interaction. Particularly, I'm excited about how we can design AI systems so that humans can easily interact and use such systems.

Proir to this I completed my undergraduate studies in Information Systems at Mekelle University by 2020. I'm co-organising the Development working group at Machanism Design for Social Good.
