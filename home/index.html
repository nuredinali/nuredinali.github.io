ABOUT ME

Hi there! I am a third year PhD student at the Universiy of Minnesota advised by Stevie Chancellor part of the Grouplens lab. My interest broadly lies at the intersection of Machine Learning and Human Computer Interaction. Particularly, I'm excited about how we can design AI systems so that humans can easily interact and use such systems. Currently, I'm focusing on harmful content analysis and detection from social media on low resourced settings. How we can design content moderation tools that acheive the needs for low resourced languages.

Proir to this I completed my undergraduate studies in Information Systems at Mekelle University by 2020. I'm co-organising the Development working group at Machanism Design for Social Good .

In my free time I love watching and playing football (soccer).
